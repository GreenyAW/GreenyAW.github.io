What is Legend of Zelda (Fan Made)

Legend of Zelda (Fan Made) is a parody of Legend of Zelda Majora's mask, but instead of the moon falling and helping people, your task is too collect as many things as you can eventually turning into dark link and destroy anything that stops you from your path to destroy Hyrule.
